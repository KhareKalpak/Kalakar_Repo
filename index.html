About Kalakar

Kalakar is Pune's premier networking platform designed specifically for the entertainment industry. Similar to LinkedIn but tailored for actors, directors, and producers, Kalakar bridges the gap between talent and opportunity.

Whether you're an aspiring actor looking to build your portfolio and get discovered, or an established director seeking fresh talent, Kalakar connects the Pune entertainment community. We facilitate connections for plays, dramas, soaps, documentaries, and other entertainment ventures.

Our mission is to empower local talent, promote Pune's vibrant cultural scene, and create sustainable opportunities for everyone in the entertainment ecosystem.
